how it works ?

  1. Download list of largest companies from NASDAQ / NYSE / AMEX ([100;500] companies)

  2. Download quotation from the opening of the stock exchange until the closing

  3. update of stock market data every minute

  4. calculation of the indicators in the stratégy (example: RSI, SMA and stochastic in parameter 14)

  5. Check the stragey for every last quotation

  6. if a trend is detected
    1. save data at this time instrument (instru name, timestamp, prices)
    2. change strategy of this instrument by the closing action strategy implemented

  7. saving all the data of the strategies, possible preview of the results in a CSV/html page

  8. at the close of the stock exchanges:
    1. all data is saved on CSV files
    2. analysis of results is now possible with CSV viewer or with the html page

STRATEGY EXAMPLE

TAKE PLACEMENT

  • RSI <30 & STOCHASTIC <20 & SMA which goes above one of the last 3 prices
    expected bullish reversal

  • RSI> 70 & STOCHASTIC> 80 & SMA which goes below one of the last 3 prices
    expected bearish reversal

  • RSI / STOCHASTIC / SMA which does not take the desired values
    no expected return

CLOSE PLACEMENT

  • the long-term sma curve is reversed compared to the previous trend
